We weren't kidding. How Special is about to genetically wipe out the industrial engine of this world and burn it a thousand million times. Take shelter graphic designers of evil fabric. We must set an indefinite end to unintentional design, the endless unconscious arrangements of compositions and the disregard of negative spaces.
The design of the brand is an ongoing process as How Special is currently being developed. The idea is a central hub in form of a collection that covers multiple arches of crafts including music, graphic design and videos.
